Getting to Taniti

Almost all visitors arrive in Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Bus Services

Public buses serve Taniti City daily, while private buses connect the rest of the island.

Taxi Services

Convenient taxi transportation is available throughout Taniti City.

Rental Cars

Rent a vehicle near the airport to explore the island at your own pace.

Bike Rentals

Bikes and helmets are available from local vendors. Helmets are required by law.

Walking

Taniti City and Merriton Landing are flat, walkable areas ideal for exploring on foot.
