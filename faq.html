Visitor Information

What voltage are the power outlets?

Power outlets are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

When is alcohol sold?

Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

What is the drinking age?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18 and is not strictly enforced.

Is English spoken on Taniti?

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas.

Are medical services available?

There is one hospital and several clinics. The hospital has multilingual employees.

Is Taniti safe?

Violent crime is rare, though pickpocketing and petty crime have increased with tourism.

Are businesses open on holidays?

Many attractions and restaurants close on national holidays.

What currency does Taniti use?

The U.S. dollar is used, though many businesses also accept euros and yen.
